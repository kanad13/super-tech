    Privacy-First AI Chatbots¶

    • Flexible Integration: Compatible with OpenAI, Llama, Claude, and other LLM backends
    • Custom Deployment: On-premise or cloud hosting options
    • Enterprise-Ready: SOC 2 and GDPR compliant
